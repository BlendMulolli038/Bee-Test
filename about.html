Our Story

Bee Solution was founded in 2020 by two passionate web designers who saw a need for affordable, high-quality web solutions for small businesses.

Just like bees work together to create something sweet, we collaborate with our clients to build digital solutions that help their businesses grow.

What started as a side project quickly grew into a full-fledged agency as word spread about our attention to detail, creative approach, and commitment to client success.

Our Journey

2020 - The Beginning

Founded Bee Solution with our first client, a local coffee shop needing a website redesign.

2021 - First Hire

Expanded our team to handle growing demand, bringing on a developer and project manager.

2022 - Award Winning

Received "Best Small Business Web Design" award from the local business association.

2023 - New Office

Moved into our new downtown office space to accommodate our growing team.
